Seekers: The Quest Begins is a book by Erin Hunter. It is about three bears, living in seperate parts of the world. One of them is Kallik, the polar bear. She lives on the ice with her mother, Nisa, and her playful brother, Toklo. When the ice melts earlier than usual, the three set out for dry land. When disaster strikes, Kallik must go out on her own into the wild. The second bear is Toklo. He is a brown bear living with his mother and very ill brother.
